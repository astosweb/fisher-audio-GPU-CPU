Local text-to-speech on Apple Silicon. Click tags to insert inline controls, or type your own like [whisper in small voice].

Script

For two-person dialogue, put both turns in one text with <|speaker:0|> and <|speaker:1|> — not separate generations. Speaker 2–4 only work with cloned reference audio assigned to that slot.

Place tags immediately before the words they affect — e.g. [whispering] keep this quiet. Tags like [pause] and [laughing] produce extra audio, so longer scripts may need a higher max-token setting.
